InterviewPrep App

Welcome to InterviewPrep, your ultimate companion for acing job interviews!

Key Features

  • Database Management for Questions and Answers
  • Live Interview Features with Real-time Feedback
  • Mock Interview Simulations
  • Resume Management and Optimization
  • AI-powered Job Search Tools

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: PyQt5
  • Backend: Flask/Django with SQLite/PostgreSQL
  • AI/ML: NLTK, SpaCy, Scikit-learn
  • Speech Recognition: SpeechRecognition library
